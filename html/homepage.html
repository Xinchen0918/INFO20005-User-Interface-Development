About us

INNOVATIVE CAFE X MODERN ASIAN AUSTRALIAN

RESTAURANT IN BOX HILL

Whether you’re in morning mode or night mode, you’re always in ZeroMode with us.

From darkness into dawn, so too do we provide unique fusion meals to tantalise the taste buds.

Zero Mode is a modern Australian cafe during the day and fusion eatery by night.We’ve taken all the Asian Fusion you’ve ever known and thrown the rule book out the door.

COMBINING THE VERY BEST AUSTRALIAN INGREDIENTS, OUR RANGE OF EXTRAORDINARY DISHES ARE CRAFTED TO HEIGHTEN YOUR DINING EXPERIENCE.

Our mode is vibrant, eclectic, unexpected. We’ll grill fresh pineapple with woody cinnamon; mix up dragon fruit and bee pollen; twice cook our wagyu and serve it with sesame leaves and yuzu.

Zero Mode was born out of a desire to create something dynamic and eclectic to transform the dining experience through day to night.
